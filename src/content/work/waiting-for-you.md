Henry & Fiona



Some stories just take a little longer to find the right moment.



Henry was the one who first reached out to me. It’s rare to see a husband-to-be carefully planning a photoshoot, and I could immediately feel how much love was behind it.



They’ve been together since their student days. In fact, Henry first contacted me after his proposal, hoping to document their journey. We missed each other a few times because of scheduling, but somehow, when they were expecting their little one, everything finally aligned.



Maybe that’s the beauty of fate.



The right people always meet, just at the right time.
